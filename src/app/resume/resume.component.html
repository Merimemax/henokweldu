Title: Bayesian Decision Theoretic Framework for Evaluating the Clinical Utility of Predictive Biomarkers and Sample Size Estimation Methods.

Objective: Develop Bayesian decision theoretic framework study design, novel estimation equations and algorithms for evaluating the clinical utility of predictive biomarkers and propose a sample size estimation method for determining the mean confidence interval width of the developed metrics.
